About Me

Where do I even begin? For starters I currently live in a town named after a famous show called: Riverdale. I have lived here most of my life. I am currenty working for a great program that assist ederly people to have their homes weatherized. I am forever greatful that I have an awesome support team: my family, small circle of friends and an amazing GF. Growing up I had always been dealt a bad hand but made the best bets in life. I can proudly state that I was considered at one point a "Dreamer". I found myself stuck at times but never gave up. It gave me the courage to step up my game and step out of my comfort zone. With that being said, I have had an opportunity of a life time: studying,working,traveling; at one point I even ended up in Seattle living my best life! I went from working hard to teaching myself how to work smarter. From being hard headed to hard working. I have not stop grinding since 2004! I was told I have a nitch when it comes to helping people. I am pationate when it comes to: Music(playing my guitar),Technology(phones and PC's),Automobiles (specifically Toyota Corolla and Jeeps), and as of recent:tiny homes! I hope that with this new skill I will be able to combine my passions to help me reach my goal of traveling, leveling up and helping as many people along the way.
